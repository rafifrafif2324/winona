The Way You Bloom



you bloom like a flower the sun waits for

soft and certain,

unfolding in your own rhythm,

never rushing, never needing to.

you carry warmth the way petals carry morning dew

quiet, delicate, full of life.

you are beauty in motion,

not loud, not seeking,

just being

and somehow, that’s more powerful than anything loud could ever be.

your kindness grows wild,

like a garden untouched,

soft enough to comfort,

strong enough to root hearts.

you don’t just brighten rooms,

you change the air in them

like jasmine in spring,

like the first bloom after winter.

you make the world gentler just by existing.

you don’t even know

but you’ve been blooming all this time.

and i’ve been lucky just to witness it.
