A Journey to Remember

i’ve seen you grow

not taller, not older

but fuller.

like time shaped you gently, even when it wasn’t kind.

like you carried the weight and still found ways to bloom.

i know it hasn’t always been easy.

the quiet battles, the lonely nights,

the pressure you wore like skin.

but you kept moving.

you kept choosing softness in a world that begged you to harden.

that’s strength, you know

real strength.

and now here you are

a whole woman.

a graduate, a fighter, a dreamer.

you’ve achieved things you once only whispered to yourself late at night.

you made it here,

and that’s not luck

that’s you.

so no, it’s not surprising when more good things come your way.

you’ve earned them.

you’ve built the life that’s starting to love you back.

and you’re just getting started.

you’ve come so far.

and i hope you see it

the beauty in your journey,

the strength in your softness,

the light you’ve always carried,

even when you couldn’t see it.

i’m proud of you.

always.
