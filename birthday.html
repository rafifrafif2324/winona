May 23 wrap you in something tender.

Like silk sheets on skin,

Like the kind of love that lingers in the air after you’ve left the room.

You’ve always been good at loving

The way you care,

The way you carry people’s hearts like they’re delicate things,

The way you listen like you’re memorizing someone’s favorite song.

And I watch you bloom quietly,

Beautifully,

Like a slow-motion sunrise.

You don’t even know how radiant you are.

But I see it.

I feel it.

I hope this year brings you closer to everything your heart wants

Not just dreams, but the kind of peace that holds your hand when no one else can.

I want the world to be kind to you.

Gentle with you.

I want the universe to whisper: you deserve all of it.

I love you in ways I haven’t even found words for yet.

But I’m trying.

I always will.
